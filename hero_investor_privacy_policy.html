Hero Investor - Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Hero Investor is developed by Smartatum (“we”, “us”, or “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Hero Investor app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Device Information: such as your device model, operating system, and language settings.
  • Usage Data: gameplay events, session duration, and in-game actions (used to improve gameplay and fix bugs).
  • Advertising Data: anonymized ad performance and engagement data (through Google AdMob and other ad partners).
  • Purchase Data: information about in-app purchases (IAPs) to deliver the purchased content correctly.

2. How We Use the Information

  • To improve gameplay experience and balance the in-game economy.
  • To fix bugs, crashes, or technical issues via analytics reports.
  • To show relevant ads in compliance with user consent preferences.
  • To process and confirm in-app purchases securely via Google Play.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob – for in-game ads and analytics. (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Google Play Billing – for secure IAP transactions.
  • Firebase Analytics – to track bugs and improve performance.

4. Data Retention and Security

We do not collect personal data such as name or email within the app. All analytics and ad data are anonymized. Information is retained only as long as necessary to provide and improve the service.
