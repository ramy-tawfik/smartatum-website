Privacy Policy for Hero Investor

Last updated: November 3, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Smartatum Software Studio (“Company”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile game Hero Investor (the “App” or “Service”), available on Google Play.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Data

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name or email unless you voluntarily provide it (for example, through contacting support).

b. Usage Data

Automatically collected when you use the App. This includes:

  • Device information (model, OS version, unique identifiers)
  • App activity and usage statistics
  • IP address (may be used to determine approximate location)
  • In-game actions (purchases, gameplay progress, session duration)
  • Crash and performance reports (Firebase)

c. Purchase Data

When you make an In-App Purchase (IAP), Google Play processes payment information on our behalf. We do not have access to your credit card details or payment account information.

d. Advertising Data

We use Google AdMob to display ads. Ad networks may use cookies, device identifiers, or similar tracking technologies to show personalized ads (based on your consent preferences).

For users in the EU and USA, consent for personalized ads is handled through Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) to comply with GDPR and CCPA.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide and maintain the App
  • Improve gameplay and performance
  • Analyze player behavior and trends
  • Display ads and measure performance
  • Process purchases and prevent fraud
  • Communicate updates or respond to support requests

3. Sharing Your Information

We may share data with service providers used in the App:

  • Firebase (Google LLC) — analytics, crash reporting
  • Google AdMob — advertisements
  • Google Play Billing — payment processing

We do not sell, trade, or rent users’ personal information.

4. Data Retention

We retain usage data as long as necessary to provide and improve the App. Players may request deletion of data linked to their device by contacting us.

5. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in other countries, including the United States. We ensure legal protections are followed.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
